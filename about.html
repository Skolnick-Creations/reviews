Movies & TV Games


Film is a medium that has been part of my life for basically as long as I can remember. One of the earliest stories that has been passed down to me is how by the time I was two years old, I had watched every single episode of the Twilight Zone from the comfort of my playpen. Growing up in the kind of home that I did, there was hardly a moment when a James Bond movie, Jimmy Stewart classic, or Hitchcock thriller wasn’t playing on my living room television. Watching these from a young age fueled my passion for talking about, analyzing, and discussing movies. I have always wanted some place to put down my ideas, and so I finally decided to create a website from scratch. There is no schedule or grand plan for it, but simply whenever I have something that I am itching to talk about whether it be tv show, movie, or even video game. Stay tuned for more…
